I was so close to giving up on the one career I was born for.

Tyron, 23 years old, Cape Town

So I started my acting career back in 2018, and it started off amazing. I was booking jobs, and really growing my career in a positive way.

Then things went dry, and for the next year and a half I was getting nowhere with my career.

This came to the point where I was heavily considering a career change, as income quickly became a problem.

I accepted a job to go overseas and work full time. Then Covid-19 happened, and that job offer went away. But, the beauty of it all is that this gave me the opportunity to really sit and think about what I truly love to do, and that was acting.

So I went onto Online Castings to see what the site is all about, and found a student film audition.

I auditioned, got the part, and during the 5 days of shooting I rediscovered my absolute love for acting and had the most fun during a shoot I could have ever imagined. When we wrapped on that project, I took a look at my career and figured out exactly what I needed to do in order to build my career once again.

Now, today I look back at that day when I went onto Online Castings, and I get emotional. I was so close to giving up on the one career I was born for.

So thanks to Online Castings for providing a place for all within the film industry.

Kind Regards Tyron
